The Climate Fresk


The climate fresk is a workshop which made us ask various questions on important social and environmental issues.
The goal is to understand how causes and consequences of climate change are linked together. They are represented by cards that we have arranged together by respecting the chronology of events.

The Sulitest


It is a test subdivided on topics such as transition towards sustainability or global and local human-constructed systems. It aims to evaluate our knowledge on sustainability literacy to increase our awareness of sustainable development. It is essential to take informed decisions in line with the respect of the environment. It taught me that there are domains where I should to learn about and enables me to have a wider vision on the subject.

Theatre piece
"the King-Kong's trial"


I participated to an interactive play that invited us to take place in a court. Indeed, the judge of this somewhat special institution, called on the public to help him understand and judge. We were therefore faced with three cases in which the culprits take themselves for the victims, but more seriously, the victims think they are guilty. Axel, Alex and Omar had three crazy integration nights at their new school. In addition to the different hazing that they would have liked to avoid, each in turn found themselves in a position of having a harassing, sexist or racist behaviour. But none of them can see when they were guilty, victims, accomplices or simply innocent.This extraordinary court has an advantage, the protagonists play live the various incriminated moments. And the public, through a moving debate, can clearly position itself in space and change its position as the exchanges and arguments are shared.

It was interesting because it made us aks to ourselves important questions on subject that could happen to us.

Cordées de la réussite


I am animating with other engineer students and professors, volunteer workshop which aims to provide support to younger students in their orientation process. The objective is to introduce greater social equity in access to higher education.
